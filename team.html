Teaching Experience:

Assistant Professor at National PG College (University of Lucknow). Previously served as Assistant Professor at Arya Mahila PG College, Banaras Hindu University (BHU), Varanasi, from August 2017 to November 2022. Over 6–7 years of experience teaching UG and PG students.

Research & Startup Projects:

Actively engaged in minor research projects and initiatives related to entrepreneurship, innovation, and startup development. Published numerous peer-reviewed research papers in reputed UGC Group-II journals.
