Department of Commerce (E-Cell), NPGC Lucknow & IID presents

SAARANG '25.

Entrepreneurial Pitch League (EPL) — the signature two-day high-octane entrepreneurial festival of National Post Graduate College, Lucknow. Bringing India's visionary student projects in direct sync with mentors, strategists, and angel incubation grants.

Official Overview

The Department of Commerce, National Post Graduate College, Lucknow, in collaboration with the Institute for Industrial Development (IID), successfully hosted SAARANG '25 on March 20th and 21st, 2025. This grand two-day corporate festival operated as a powerful nexus to elevate student innovations into functional commercial companies.

Under the advisory of regional industrial headers, SaaS builders, and academic coordinators, SAARANG '25 provided real capital, seed validation courses, and free incubation grants to all participants, laying down a robust roadmap for prospective student empires.

🛡️ IID Incubation Commitment

CA Mukesh Shukla announced that every single participant of EPL will receive free operational incubation backing from IID to systematically mature their business formulations.

🛡️ Government Support Session

The summit hosted an exclusive awareness layout detailing the CM Yuva Udyami Yojana, equipping collegiate builders with frameworks to claim central state grants.

Festival Highlights

Trace the fundamental milestones that made the SAARANG '25 Entrepreneurial League exceptionally valuable:

🚀

EPL Pitching Showdown

More than 50 business models were pitched live and online via hybrid channels by passionate innovators representing over 30 leading Indian academies.

🏪

40+ Student Stalls

A highly vibrant, real-world retail arena set up by student sellers, leading banks, and specialized suppliers showcasing consumer and culinary design.

⚖️

Luminary Jury Debates

Tense investor evaluations hosted by leading ISRO scientists, commerce experts, and MMMS administrative keys critiquing business viability.

📈

Udyami Yojana Briefings

High-engagement seminar explaining the functional mechanics, eligibility, and scale-up models of the CM Yuva Udyami Yojana.

🛡️

Democratic Incubation

Universal free incubation certificates delivered by the Institute for Industrial Development for all active team pitch entries.

🏆

Best Stall Evaluations

Direct business inspections looking at day-to-day profit models, customer engagement, and inventory logs in competitive student stalls.
