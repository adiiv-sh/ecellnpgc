About the Cell

We don't just teach entrepreneurship — we live it.

UDYAM (Unleashing Dreams of Young Aspirants Through Mentorship) was founded with a singular vision: to empower student entrepreneurs at National Post Graduate College, Lucknow. We believe that every student has the potential to create something extraordinary.

Since our inception, we have been instrumental in helping students transform their ideas into business realities. Through quality mentorship programs, workshops, networking events, and hands-on support, we have created an ecosystem where entrepreneurial dreams can flourish.

Today, UDYAM stands as a thriving community of innovators, dreamers, and doers who are dedicated to making a positive impact on the entrepreneurial landscape.

Value & Opportunities

Be part of a thriving community of innovators and entrepreneurs.

For Student Entrepreneurs

  • Mentorship: Get guidance from experienced business leaders and successful entrepreneurs who have built ventures from scratch.
  • Skill Development: Participate in workshops covering marketing, finance, product development, and other essential skills.
  • Networking: Connect with like-minded students, investors, and industry professionals.

  • Monthly Workshops: Expert-led sessions on business fundamentals, startup strategies, and industry trends.

  • Pitch Events: Opportunities to present your ideas to investors, founders, and peers.

Our Journey

Milestones & Achievements

From humble beginnings to becoming a thriving entrepreneurial hub.

2020

Foundation Year

UDYAM was founded with a vision to foster entrepreneurship among students. We started with a small group of passionate individuals and a dream to make a difference.

2021

First Startup Summit

We organized our inaugural startup summit, bringing together 200+ students, mentors, and investors. This event marked the beginning of our large-scale impact.

2022

Mentor Network Expansion

We grew our mentor network to include 15+ experienced entrepreneurs and business leaders. These mentors have guided numerous student ventures.

2023

8 Startups Launched

Eight startups founded by our members successfully launched their operations. These ventures represent diverse sectors and innovative solutions.

2024

Community Growth

Our community grew to 50+ active members. We conducted 12+ workshops, 10+ mentorship sessions, and various networking events throughout the year.

2025

SAARANG '25 (EPL)

NPGC E-Cell partnered with IID to host SAARANG '25, an Entrepreneurial Pitch League with a ₹93,000+ prize pool, drawing over 50+ business pitches from 30+ colleges nationwide.

2025

CM YUVA Exhibition

UDYAM E-Cell collaborated with the UP State Government to organize student participation at the massive CM YUVA Exhibition at Indira Gandhi Pratishthan, connecting students with over 120+ micro-industrial and technology exhibitors.

2025

Annual Report 2025

UDYAM Cell published its Annual Report, highlighting achievements across Eureka, SAARANG '25, and acknowledging the driving force of the core team.

2025

Hunar Launchpad Experience

UDYAM E-Cell hosted the Hunar Launchpad briefing program, introducing a structured 6–7 month internship program for second-year students, attended by 250+ students.

2026

AKTU Incubation Visit

National PG College E-Cell organized a guided industrial visit to the Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU) Incubation Centre, connecting student innovators with incubation resources and live innovation labs.
