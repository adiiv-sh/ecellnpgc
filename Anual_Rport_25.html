Annual Report Summary

The Entrepreneurship Cell (UDYAM CELL) at National PG College, under the Department of Commerce, successfully championed innovation and business acumen this year. Through our flagship events, Eureka and SAARANG '25, we provided dynamic platforms for young innovators to present their startup ideas to industry experts and investors, gaining practical insights into the startup ecosystem.

EUREKA

Sparking the Entrepreneurial Spirit

Aimed at encouraging problem-solving and creative thinking, Eureka successfully pushed students out of their comfort zones to showcase their entrepreneurial ventures.

📈 Impact & Milestones

The event marked a new milestone with six diverse teams participating. Most notably, one participating team successfully launched their own startup after the event, reflecting the true spirit of the competition.

🚀 Innovative Pitches

Students pitched unique, out-of-the-box ideas such as Refab Ceramics (creating marbles from waste clothes), Simulation Sandloo (a real-life challenge game for entrepreneurs), and Anant Path (innovative funeral services).

🛡️ Expert Insights

Sponsored directly by National PG College, the event featured prominent speakers including Mr. Amit Sinha, Mr. Amit Agarwal (who highlighted opportunities under the U.P. CM Yuva Udyam Yojana), and Mr. Anurag Mishra.

SAARANG '25

The Entrepreneurial Pitch League (EPL)

Held on March 20th and 21st, 2025, in collaboration with the Institute for Industrial Development (IID), SAARANG '25 was a massive two-day entrepreneurial fest.

🌍 Scale & Reach

The event featured over 50 business pitches presented in a hybrid mode by participants from across India, alongside over 40 diverse stalls set up by students, banks, and suppliers.

🏆 Esteemed Guests

The inaugural ceremony was graced by Dr. Mirza Mohammad Zahir, Scientist-G at ISTRAC-ISRO. The grand finale featured CA Mukesh Shukla, Director of IID, as the Chief Guest.

🤝 Incubation Support

In a major announcement, CA Mukesh Shukla committed free incubation support from IID to every single EPL participant to help develop their startup ideas into full-fledged businesses.

🏆 Top EPL Winners

  • First Prize (₹51,000): Ananta Mehra (Art Jewellery, Amity University).
  • Second Prize (₹21,000): Aashish Srivastava (Krishi Inspiro, Mysore College).
  • Third Prize (₹11,000): Anmol Srivastava & Abhinav Tripathi (Noir India, NPGC).
